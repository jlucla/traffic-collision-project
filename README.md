#Traffic Collision Analysis

Tools: SQL (Sqlite)

I analyzed traffic collision data from Los Angeles (2010 – 2025), focusing on temporal patterns and fatality risk factors.

**Key Questions:**

- How have collisions changed over time (i.e. annually)?

- What times of day have the highest collision frequency and fatality risk?

- How do fatality rates differ by collision type?

- How do contextual factors (e.g., early morning, holidays) affect fatality risk?

**Key Insights:**

- Fatal collisions tend to be concentrated in late evening and early morning hours

- Vehicle–pedestrian collisions have significantly higher fatality rates, compared to vehicle-bicycle or vehicle-vehicle collisions

- Early morning collisions and collisions occurring on federal holidays exhibit slightly elevated fatality risk
